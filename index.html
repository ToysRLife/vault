    Global Assumptions

    Milestone rules: At rebalance year, all Equity moves to FD (kills sequence-of-returns risk). At NPS unlock year, Retirement (PF+NPS) splits — 60% to FD, 40% to Pension Annuity (regulatory rule).

    Net worth = sum of investment balances (compounded with each category's ROI). Income is NOT added separately — it's assumed already allocated to Investments + Expenses. Cash-flow shortfalls draw down from balances. Click ▶ on any segment to set per-year overrides.

    Segments — Income, Investments, Expenses

    Income managed in Income tab · Expenses managed in Planned Expenses tab · Investment balances aggregated from Investments + Real Estate
    Recomputes Equity/FD/Gold/Retirement/Real Estate balances by summing the underlying holdings.
